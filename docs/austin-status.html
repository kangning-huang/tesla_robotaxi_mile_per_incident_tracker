What "Unsupervised" Means

Austin is Tesla's ONLY location with true unsupervised robotaxi operations:

  • No human safety driver in the vehicle
  • Level 4 ADS (Automated Driving System) classification
  • NHTSA reporting required under Standing General Order 2021-01
  • Paid commercial service available to customers

This is different from the Bay Area fleet, which operates with safety drivers under California regulations.
